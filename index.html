About Our App

church of christ Ghana

This application provides the complete hymn collection of the Church of Christ, including both Twi and English versions. It also features a Favorites section and other essential church-related information.

How to Use

  • Tap the Three horizontal lines at top left corner inside the hymns section to find hymns.
  • Search by text or hymn number, including Twi characters such as ɛ, ɔ, ā, ń, é, etc.
  • Copy hymns and paste them anywhere for easy sharing.
  • Check for updates by tapping the Update button.
  • No internet connection is required to use the app.

Enjoy the app, and feel free to use and share!
